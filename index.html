About Maximus Buildcon

Building Material Distribution, Reimagined

Maximus Buildcon is an integrated building materials supply and distribution company with a network spanning over 400 retailers across Maharashtra and Gujarat. We supply to India's most respected developers, architects, and contractors — bridging the gap between world-class brands and landmark Indian projects.

Our portfolio caters to the full spectrum — from exclusive architect-designed homes and high-end housing projects to mass housing developments — making premium-quality materials accessible at every scale.
