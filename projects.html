Where Our Materials Live

A selection of the developers, landmarks and residences that trust Maximus Buildcon for their building materials supply.

Featured Clients

Building India's Landmarks

Supplying directly to major builders, mall developers, and premium housing projects across India.

Premium Residential · Kandivali, Mumbai

Gundecha Builders

One of Mumbai's most established residential developers, with landmark projects like Gundecha Trillium in Kandivali East. Their high-rise developments specify materials that balance design ambition with the realities of long-term Indian residential use — a balance Maximus-supplied tiles, cisterns, and bathware are built for.

Retail & Commercial · Lower Parel, Mumbai

Phoenix Palladium

India's flagship luxury retail destination — built within the heritage Phoenix Mills compound at Lower Parel. With over 5 million annual visitors, the surfaces here have to be beautiful, durable, and architecturally consistent. Our Italian and Thai tile brands deliver on all three.

High-End Housing · Mulund, Mumbai

Neelam Developers

Founded in 1978, Neelam Realtors has delivered over 3.6 million sq ft of residential, commercial and township projects across Mumbai — including Neelam Supremo, Neelam Solstice, and Neelam Neapean Sea Road. Their curated materials palette is one we've helped specify across multiple landmark towers.

Heritage & Industrial · Ballard Estate, Mumbai

Walchand Group

Founded by industrialist Walchand Hirachand — pioneer of India's first modern shipyard, aircraft factory and car factory — the Walchand Group remains a legacy name in Indian infrastructure. A long-standing Maximus Buildcon client, our portfolio supports both their heritage commercial properties and contemporary developments.

Commercial High-Rise · Lower Parel, Mumbai

Marathon Futurex (Marathon Realty)

An iconic IGBC gold-rated commercial high-rise at Mafatlal Mills, Lower Parel — over 1.1 million sq ft of premium office space across multiple towers, with floor plates up to 65,000 sq ft and 15+ sky gardens. Marathon's signature is scale without compromise on quality — and our distribution capacity supports their volume without losing specification detail.
