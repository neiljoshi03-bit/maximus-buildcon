Hamlet Tiles

Our own brand — manufactured at a top-tier Morbi facility — specialising in marble inlay, high-gloss and matte flex bodies, and Moroccan designs.

Concept

Marble Inlay, Made in India

Hamlet is Maximus Buildcon's in-house tile brand — conceived to bring marble-inlay aesthetics and Moroccan decorative traditions into modern Indian homes, at a level of quality usually reserved for imported surfaces.

Produced in Morbi — the world's largest ceramic manufacturing hub — at a top-tier facility under our direct supervision, Hamlet combines design ambition with the advantages of local manufacturing: faster delivery, deeper inventory, and scale.
