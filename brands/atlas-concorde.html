Atlas Concorde

The world's largest tile conglomerate. Exclusively imported and distributed in India by Maximus Buildcon.

The Brand

A Legacy of Italian Craft

Founded in the tile-making heartland of Modena, Italy, Atlas Concorde represents the pinnacle of porcelain and ceramic tile craftsmanship. With decades of research, design collaborations with world-class architects, and an uncompromising commitment to aesthetic purity, the brand is a defining presence in global architecture.

As the exclusive Indian import and distribution partner, Maximus Buildcon makes the full Atlas Concorde portfolio available to India's most discerning architects, developers and homeowners — with direct technical support and timely project delivery.
