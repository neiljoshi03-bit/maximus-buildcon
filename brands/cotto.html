Cotto

The world's second-largest tile maker — now Made in India with Thailand's quality standards and design heritage.

The Brand

Thai Craft, Indian Manufacturing

Cotto — the tile division of Siam Cement Group, one of Asia's largest industrial conglomerates — is the world's second-largest tile manufacturer. Its reputation is built on consistent quality control, innovative surface design, and an instantly recognizable aesthetic sensibility.

Cotto is now Made in India — combining Thailand's quality standards and design DNA with the scale, speed, and cost-efficiency of domestic manufacturing. Maximus Buildcon distributes Cotto's exclusive collection of stone, marble, mosaic and pool tiles.
