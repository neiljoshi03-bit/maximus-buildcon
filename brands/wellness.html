Bullfrog Spas

Personalised Hydrotherapy, at Home

Bullfrog is the world's only spa brand with patented JetPak Therapy — an interchangeable jet system that lets homeowners customise their massage experience across multiple seats. It's spa technology reimagined, and it's what makes Bullfrog the choice of serious wellness seekers.

Energy-efficient, engineered for quiet operation, and built with plumbing fewer than 90% of traditional spas, Bullfrog spas are designed for the way modern homes actually use them.

Harvia Saunas

The Finnish Sauna, Authentic

Harvia is Finland's — and the world's — largest sauna manufacturer. With a century of expertise in wood-burning and electric sauna systems, Harvia delivers the authentic Finnish sauna experience across residential and commercial applications.

From compact home saunas to elaborate hammams and infrared cabins — every product is a bridge between Nordic tradition and contemporary design.

Fahrenheit Outdoor Bathtubs

Open-Air Bathing Rituals

Fahrenheit bathtubs turn outdoor spaces — weekend villas, poolside patios, rooftop gardens — into a private wellness sanctuary. Wood-fired or heater-controlled, crafted with weather-resistant materials, they are the finishing gesture of a considered outdoor design.

Ideal for luxury homes, farmhouse retreats and resort-style residences.

Mr. Steam · USA

The Luxury Ritual of Steam

Mr. Steam is the world's only dedicated luxury steam brand — a 100-year-old American name engineered for the finest spas, hotels and private residences. As part of the Fahrenheit Wellness portfolio, we are the exclusive Western India distributors.

From whisper-quiet steam generators and intuitive iSteam digital controls to AromaSteam essential-oil infusion, ChromaSteam LED lighting and AudioSteam Bluetooth integration — every Mr. Steam installation turns the home shower into a complete sensory wellness ritual.

Available as full turnkey custom steam rooms — from sizing the generator to specifying the tile.
