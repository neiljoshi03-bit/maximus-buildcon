Geberit

The world's leading Swiss brand for concealed flush tanks, with India's largest after-sales service network. Maximus Buildcon are Mumbai's main distributors.

The Brand

Swiss Precision, Engineered to Last

For over 140 years, Geberit has set the global benchmark in behind-the-wall sanitary engineering. Its concealed flush tanks are specified into the world's most demanding projects — from five-star hospitality to high-rise residential towers — for a simple reason: they work, year after year, without intervention.

As Mumbai's main distributor, Maximus Buildcon supplies the complete Geberit range and backs it with India's largest after-sales service network — ensuring that products perform for the lifespan of the building.
