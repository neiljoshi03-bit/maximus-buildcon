Who We Are

An Integrated Distribution Vision

Bridging the gap between the world's most respected building material brands and India's most ambitious developers, architects and homeowners.

Our Story

Built on Trust, Delivered with Distinction

Maximus Buildcon began with a single conviction — that India's design ambitions deserved unrestricted access to the world's finest building materials. Today, we operate as an integrated supply and distribution company, with a network of over 400 retail partners spanning Maharashtra and Gujarat.

From flagship malls and luxury residences to mass housing developments, our materials shape the way India is built. We supply directly to leading developers including Gundecha Construction, Phoenix Mall, Neelam Developers, Walchand Group and Marathon Realty.

Our success stems from a simple philosophy — represent only the best, support partners with depth, and ensure every product we distribute carries the legacy of its maker.

What We Do

End-to-End Material Sourcing

We don't just import and resell. We advise architects on specifications, support developers across multi-tower projects, train retail partners on product education, and hold inventory ready for India's project timelines.

  • Exclusive Indian distribution rights for global brands
  • Western India distribution for premium bathware
  • Direct project supply to India's top developers
  • 400+ retail partner network across two states
  • Specification consulting for architects and designers
  • Two showrooms — Mumbai and Vadodara
